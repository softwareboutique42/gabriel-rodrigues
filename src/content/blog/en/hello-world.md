# Hello World!

Welcome to my blog. I'm Gabriel Rodrigues, a Senior Frontend Engineer with over 10 years of experience building web applications across Europe and Latin America.

## What to expect

Here I'll be sharing my thoughts and experiences on:

- **Frontend Architecture** - Patterns and practices for building scalable applications with Angular and Vue

After years of learning from the amazing web development community, I want to give back. Writing helps me organize my thoughts, and I hope my experiences can help others facing similar challenges.

Stay tuned for more posts!
