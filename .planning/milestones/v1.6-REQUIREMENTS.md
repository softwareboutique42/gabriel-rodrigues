## v1.6 Requirements

### Slots Shell and Presentation

- [x] **VIS-20**: Slots routes present a more polished shell with stronger visual hierarchy, reel framing, and HUD clarity on desktop and mobile.
- [x] **VIS-21**: Symbol, reel, and gameplay state presentation clearly communicates idle, spinning, win, loss, and blocked states without changing gameplay authority.
- [x] **VIS-22**: Slots gains a cohesive atmosphere layer (background, cabinet/chrome, supporting visuals) that feels production-ready and remains parity-safe in EN/PT.

### Effects and Motion

- [x] **FX-20**: Spin, stop, win, and blocked flows have richer visual effects and transitions with reduced-motion-safe fallbacks.
- [x] **FX-21**: New visual feedback remains deterministic, performance-safe, and presentation-only across canonical Slots routes.
- [x] **FX-22**: Visual theming supports at least one coherent polished variant or atmosphere pass without gameplay forks.

### Visual QA and Release Confidence

- [x] **QA-30**: Contract and compatibility coverage protect critical Slots visual-state hooks and EN/PT parity after the refresh.
- [x] **QA-31**: Release verification captures lint, targeted tests, compatibility checks, and build evidence for refreshed Slots visuals.

## v2+ Requirements (Deferred)

- **VIS-30**: Cinematic bonus sequences or attract-mode presentation layers.
- **FX-30**: Audio-reactive or shader-heavy effect systems beyond v1.6 performance budget.
